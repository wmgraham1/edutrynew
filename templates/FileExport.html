Welcome to the site where advocates for better education collaborate to improve educational achievement.

This site is intended to provide a place for advocates for better education based on the philosophies of Salman Khan and the efforts of Khan Academy to share problems and ideas and collaborate on efforts to improve educational achievement world-wide.

There are 2 major focuses/purposes to this site:

  1. A Discussion Forum where advocates can share and discuss ideas, problems and experiences. Discussion Papers, Questions, Problems, Experience Reports, etc. are divided into 4 broad categories that are represented on the main menu of the site (Learning Resources/Videos, Learning Platform, Learners/Programs, Misc Discussions).
  2. Tools for non-technical users to support the translation efforts related to making the Khan Academy Platform available in every language, including the ability to try the exercises out in different languages.
